Overview:

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a dynamic and rapidly evolving field that focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems and software. Students gain knowledge in core areas such as Algorithms, Data Structures, Databases, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Artificial Intelligence, and Web Technologies.
